# Biophysical Mechanisms

## Hypothesized Mechanisms for Omission Response

- **PV-like gain control**: Feedback modulates perisomatic inhibition to regulate post-omission gain without destroying sparse omission spiking.
- **VIP-like disinhibition**: Feedback through VIP cells reduces SST-mediated suppression, facilitating post-omission responses.
- **Nonspecific Reset**: A global excitability or noise reset following an expected event, which may increase post-omission firing but lacks specific circuit structure.

## Mechanism Validation

Mechanisms are tested by comparing model outputs (spikes, fields, spectra) against empirical standards. A mechanism is `ACCEPTED` as a candidate only if it improves the joint fit across multiple metrics compared to simpler models.
